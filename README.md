# StoreLab Growth Engine

> Relationship Intelligence for Enterprise Growth.

StoreLab Growth Engine is an AI-powered relationship intelligence platform built specifically for enterprise business development.

Unlike traditional CRMs that focus on opportunities, Growth Engine is designed around relationships, buying centres and account intelligence—helping teams understand who to engage, when to engage and why.

---

## Vision

Every morning, open one application and immediately know the highest-value actions to move your business forward.

---

## Core Principles

- Relationships before opportunities
- AI-driven recommendations
- Screenshot-first data capture
- Beautiful, distraction-free interface
- Enterprise account intelligence
- Built for modern relationship selling
